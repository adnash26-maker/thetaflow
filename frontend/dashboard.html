How ThetaFlow Works
A step-by-step breakdown of what happens when you enter a headline.
01
You Enter a Headline
Paste any news headline, earnings report, macro announcement, or "what if" scenario. ThetaFlow works with anything from "NVIDIA beats earnings by 20%" to "China bans rare earth exports."
02
AI Identifies Investment Themes
Claude AI reads the headline and identifies which investment themes (value chains) are affected. It maps the full supply chain — from upstream suppliers to downstream applications — finding companies at every layer.
03
Skips the Obvious, Finds Hidden Plays
The "obvious play" is dismissed (everyone sees that one). Instead, ThetaFlow identifies 2nd and 3rd-order effects — companies that benefit or suffer through indirect supply chain connections most investors miss.
04
Real Financial Data + Historical Precedent
For each ticker, ThetaFlow pulls live prices from Yahoo Finance, computes 30-day projection cones using Geometric Brownian Motion, and references historical precedents — what happened the last time a similar event occurred.
05
Actionable Output with Risk Assessment
Each analysis includes: BUY/SHORT/WATCH signals, price targets with risk/reward ratios, quantified earnings impact, compounding catalysts, and a contrarian view — what could go wrong.
