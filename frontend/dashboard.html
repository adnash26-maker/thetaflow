How ThetaFlow Works ❓
A step-by-step breakdown of what happens when you enter a headline.
1
You Enter a Headline
Paste any news headline, earnings report, macro announcement, or "what if" scenario. ThetaFlow works with anything from "NVIDIA beats earnings by 20%" to "China bans rare earth exports."
2
AI Identifies the Investment Themes
Claude AI reads the headline and identifies which investment themes (value chains) are affected. It then maps out the full supply chain — from upstream suppliers to downstream applications — finding companies at every layer.
3
It Skips the Obvious, Finds the Hidden Plays
The "obvious play" is dismissed (everyone sees that one). Instead, ThetaFlow identifies 2nd and 3rd-order effects — the companies that benefit or suffer through indirect supply chain connections that most investors miss.
4
Real Financial Data + Historical Precedent
For each recommended ticker, ThetaFlow pulls live prices from Yahoo Finance, computes 30-day projection cones using Geometric Brownian Motion, and references historical precedents — what happened to these stocks the last time a similar event occurred.
5
Actionable Output with Risk Assessment
Each analysis includes: BUY/SHORT/WATCH signals, price targets with risk/reward ratios, quantified earnings impact, compounding catalysts, and a contrarian view — what could go wrong. Every recommendation is tracked in the Scorecard for accountability.
